Joined using the following SQL:
```
SELECT Ftp_sales.PARCEL_ID, Ftp_sales.SALEDT, Ftp_sales.PRICE, Ftp_sales.BOOK, Ftp_sales.PAGE, Ftp_sales.GRANTOR, Ftp_sales.GRANTEE, Ftp_site.LN_NUM, Ftp_site.BLD_NUM, Ftp_site.STR_PFX, Ftp_site.STR, Ftp_site.STR_NUM, Ftp_site.STR_NUM_SFX, Ftp_site.STR_SFX_DIR, Ftp_site.STR_UNIT, Ftp_site.ZIP, Ftp_site.CITY, Ftp_parcel.DORDESC, Ftp_parcel.YR_IMPROVED
FROM (Ftp_sales INNER JOIN Ftp_site ON Ftp_sales.PARCEL_ID = Ftp_site.PARCEL_ID) INNER JOIN Ftp_parcel ON Ftp_sales.PARCEL_ID = Ftp_parcel.PARCEL_ID;
```
